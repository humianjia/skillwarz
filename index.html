How to Play SkillWarz

Controls are straightforward and easy to learn.

Play Now

Basic Controls

  • WASD = Movement
  • Space = Jump
  • C = Crouch/Slide
  • Shift = Sprint
  • Left Click = Fire
  • Right Click = Aim Down Sight

Gameplay Tips

  • Crosshair Placement: Keep it near head level to reduce aim adjustment.
  • Recoil Control: Learn how your gun kicks and compensate slightly.
  • Map Awareness: Watch minimap, use sounds and landmarks, stay aware of flanks.
