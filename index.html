WHAT IS SKILLWARZ?

SkillWarz is a fast-paced first-person shooter offering advanced movement mechanics and diverse game modes including Deathmatch, Team Deathmatch, Gun Game, Team Gun Game, Skull Hunt, Elimination, Capture The Flag, and Domination. Navigate through over 40 dynamic day and night maps featuring bounce pads, teleports, and battle pickups. Unlock unique classes, customize your gear, and challenge others or practice against bots in action-packed matches tailored to your style.

SkillWarz is a cross-platform game available on web, mobile, and desktop, allowing you to play anytime, anywhere. The game features a progression system with unlocks, statistics tracking, and a training mode where you can practice movement and shooting before entering multiplayer matches.

GAME MODES

  • Deathmatch: Free-for-all combat where every player fights for themselves
  • Team Deathmatch: Work with your team to eliminate the opposing team
  • Gun Game: Progress through different weapons with each elimination - first to cycle through all weapons wins
  • Team Gun Game: Team-based version of Gun Game
  • Skull Hunt: Unique objective-based mode combining combat with strategic elements
  • Elimination: Players have limited lives, last team standing wins
  • Capture The Flag: Capture the enemy flag while defending your own
  • Domination: Control strategic points on the map
  • Training Mode: Practice movement and shooting against AI opponents

FEATURES

  • Advanced Movement System: Run, jump, strafe, slide, and use bounce pads for dynamic gameplay
  • Cross-Platform Support: Play on web, mobile, and desktop
  • 40+ Maps: Diverse day and night maps with unique tactical opportunities
  • Progression System: Unlock new content as you play
  • Statistics Tracking: Monitor your performance and improve your skills
  • Bot Practice: Hone your skills against AI opponents
  • Customization: Personalize your character with different gear and skins
  • No Download Required: Play directly in your browser

CONTROLS

  • WASD = move
  • Space = jump
  • C = crouch/slide
  • Shift = sprint
  • R = reload weapon
  • Left click = fire weapon
  • Right click = aim down the sight
  • Mouse wheel = switch weapon
  • Q = melee
  • G = primary grenade
  • F = secondary grenade
  • 1 = select primary weapon
  • 2 = select secondary weapon
  • 3 = select melee weapon
  • 4 = use pickup bonus
  • Tab = scoreboard
  • ~ = menu
  • T = spray
  • Y = say all
  • U = say team
